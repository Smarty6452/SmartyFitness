A person's metabolism is not just one thing. It is an entire process by which your body stores and converts the foods you eat into usable energy necessary for keeping you alive and active.

10 Bad habits that may kill your metabolism are :

Skipping First meal — Eating a nutritious breakfast is a great way to start your day. Because your metabolism slows during sleep, eating can fire it up and help you burn more calories throughout the day

Sitting too much — sitting for extended periods puts your body into energy-conservation mode, which means your metabolism can suffer.

Stay Hydrated — After going several hours without a sip, a serving of water first thing can hydrate the body while aiding digestion and metabolism

Stress — When stress levels increase, your body produces a hormone called cortisol, Cortisol stimulates your fat and carbohydrate metabolism, creating a surge of energy in your body

Consuming Junk Food — Just a few days of junk food could change your metabolism
